⏰ Tense Tip

yesterday/last year/ago → Simple Past. since/for + ongoing → Present Perfect Continuous. Never mix them!

📝 Articles

'a' + consonant SOUND. 'an' + vowel SOUND. 'a university' (yoo-sound). 'an hour' (h silent). No article before sports!

🔀 Subject–Verb

he/she/it → doesn't/isn't/hasn't/was. everyone/nobody/each → singular verb. 'They was' is ALWAYS wrong.

⚠️ Indian English

Avoid: 'do the needful', 'revert back', 'gave exam', 'passed out from college', 'discuss about', 'prepone'. Not global English!

🧠 Stative Verbs

know/like/want/have(possession)
/understand/believe — NEVER use -ing. 'I am knowing' ❌ → 'I know' ✅

🔢 Countable

Countable → many/few. Uncountable → much/little/a lot of. Information/advice/furniture have NO plural form!
