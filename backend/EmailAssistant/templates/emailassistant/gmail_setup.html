How to Get Your Gmail App Password

Follow these steps on your computer or phone

Step-by-Step Guide
1
Open Google Account Security settings

Go to your Google Account and click on the Security tab in the left sidebar.

🔗 Open Google Account Security →
2
Enable 2-Step Verification (if not already ON)

Scroll down to "How you sign in to Google". Click 2-Step Verification and follow the prompts to turn it ON.

ℹ️   App Passwords only work when 2-Step Verification is enabled. If it's already ON, skip to Step 3.
3
Search for "App Passwords"

Inside the Security page, use the search bar at the top and type App Passwords — then click the result.

🔗 Direct Link: App Passwords Page →
4
Sign in again if asked

Google may ask you to confirm your password before showing App Passwords. This is normal.

5
Create a new App Password

In the "App name" box, type a name like NEXA HUB AI — then click Create.

6
Copy the 16-character password

Google will show a yellow box with a 16-character password (like abcd efgh ijkl mnop). Copy it — you will only see it once!

✅   Copy the full password including or excluding spaces — both work, we strip spaces automatically.
7
Paste it below and click Save

Come back to this page and paste the copied password in the form below.
