⏰ Tense Tip

Use Past Simple with 'yesterday/last week'. Use Present Perfect with 'since/for/just'. Never mix them!

📝 Articles

'a' + consonant sound, 'an' + vowel sound. Exception: 'a university' (u=yoo sound), 'an hour' (h silent).

🔀 Subject-Verb

She/He/It → doesn't, isn't, wasn't, hasn't. They/We/I/You → don't, aren't, weren't, haven't.

⚠️ Indian English

Avoid: 'do the needful', 'revert back', 'gave exam', 'discuss about'. These are not standard English!
