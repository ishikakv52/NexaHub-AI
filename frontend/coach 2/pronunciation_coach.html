🎯 TH Sound

Place tongue between teeth, blow softly. "Three" not "tree".

🎯 V vs W

V: bite lip, W: round lips. "Very" not "wery".

🎯 Schwa Sound

Unstressed 'a' in 'about' → /ə/. Relax the mouth.

🎯 Word Stress

DEvelop vs deVELop. Stress changes meaning.
