How to Get Your App Password

Follow these 7 simple steps

Step-by-Step Guide
1
Open Google Account Security

Go to your Google Account and click on the Security tab in the left sidebar.

🔗 Open Google Account Security →
2
Enable 2-Step Verification

Scroll to "How you sign in to Google". Click 2-Step Verification and turn it ON.

ℹ️ App Passwords only work with 2-Step Verification enabled. Skip this if already ON.
3
Search for "App Passwords"

Use the search bar and type App Passwords, then click the result.

🔗 Direct Link: App Passwords →
4
Sign in Again (if prompted)

Google may ask you to confirm your password for security — this is normal.

5
Create a New App Password

In the "App name" field, type NEXA HUB AI, then click Create.

6
Copy the 16-Character Password

Google will display a yellow box with a 16-character password like abcd efgh ijkl mnop. Copy it — you'll only see it once!

✅ Spaces are fine — we'll strip them automatically.
7
Paste & Save Below
