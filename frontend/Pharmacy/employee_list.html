Employees Guide
Step-by-Step Guide
  1. Use the search box to filter by first name, last name, or Employee ID.
  2. Click 'Add Employee' to create a new record — this also creates the person's login account.
  3. Click any employee's name to open their full profile and employment history.
  4. The pencil icon opens the edit form for that employee.
