Point of Sale Guide
Step-by-Step Guide
  1. Search or scan a barcode in the box above the medicine grid to filter items quickly.
  2. Click a medicine tile to add it to the cart — click again to increase quantity.
  3. Pick an existing customer from the dropdown, or type a new Name + Mobile directly — it's saved automatically on checkout.
  4. Choosing UPI or Online as the payment method shows a live QR code — the amount updates automatically as the cart changes.
  5. For UPI/Online, click 'Payment Received — Complete Sale' only after you've confirmed the payment in your bank/UPI app (this keeps sales accurate — no gateway is connected to auto-verify).
  6. For Cash/Card, just click 'Complete Sale' to finalize — this deducts stock from the batch automatically.
